My Favorite Recipe: Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 50g fresh basil leaves
  • 30g pine nuts
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 50ml olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  • Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  • In a blender, combine basil, pine nuts, garlic, Parmesan, and olive oil until smooth.
  • Drain the pasta and mix with the pesto sauce.
  • Season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.
